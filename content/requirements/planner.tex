\subsection{Planner}

    \subitem{
        The purpose of the Planner is to generate a series of realizable states for the UAV. 
        The planner allows for both autonomous and pilot-directed operation. Inputs from both autonomous
        and pilot-directed modes are evaluated and potentially adjusted to fit safety and feasibility constraints. 
        The inputs are evaluated based on the current UAV state and the current tolerances from configuration parameters.
    }

    \subsubsection{Non-Functional Requirements}

        \textbf{Energy}
        \begin{enumerate}[(a)]
            \item The Planner shall operate within the power budget allocated for the onboard processor.
        \end{enumerate}

        \textbf{Environments}
        \begin{enumerate}[(a)]
            \item The Planner shall tolerate expected operating temperatures and vibration levels.
        \end{enumerate}

        \textbf{Safety}
        \begin{enumerate}[(a)]
            \item The Planner shall not issue unsafe actuator commands under fault conditions.
        \end{enumerate}

        \textbf{Structure}
        \begin{enumerate}[(a)]
            \item The Planner shall expose well-defined APIs for its submodules.
        \end{enumerate}

        \textbf{Standards and Regulations}
        \begin{enumerate}[(a)]
            \item The Planner shall follow applicable coding and safety guidelines.
